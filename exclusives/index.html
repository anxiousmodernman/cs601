SmartBrief exclusives are opportunities for our advertising sponsors to own the message and block out the competition. Opportunities range from letting us handle all the content - the industry news our subscribers know and love - to a premium opportunity for advertisers to provide their own message to our audience of millions of industry professionals.

Special Reports Sponsored Features
The Special Report is a 1 or 2 part email send to an entire e-newsletter audience. The Sponsored Feature is an opportunity for advertisers to send advertorial content to the SmartBrief audience.
Best Of Dedicated Send
The Best Of send captures the most important and interesting stories shared in a specific SmartBrief during the calendar year. The Dedicated Send is an opportunity for a single sponsor to send a 100% ad-content email to one of our professional audience segments.
